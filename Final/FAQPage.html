Frequently Asked Questions!

Q: My tattoo is itchy! Should I be worried?

A: No :D it is normal for your tattoo to be itchy later in the healing process. However, if it gets enflamed or begins oozing, get to the doctor ASAP! Those are signs of infection!

Q: Is getting a tattoo expensive?

A: It depends on the artist and how much time it will take to complete your tattoo. Our artists average around $150 an hour, with a minimum of $100. We also ask for a 50% deposit.

Q: Do you do walk-ins?

A: Typically, no. Though we have on occasion. Keep up with our Instagram to keep an eye out! Otherwise, contact us for an appointment!

Q: I can't make my appointment! What should I do?

A: Call us ASAP! Explain your situation to the receptionist and hopefully your artist can reschedule you ASAP! Our artists are understanding of unavoidable situations.
